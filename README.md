# Mahesha Automation

AI agent infrastructure for automating back-office operations in local service businesses. Lead response, missed lead recovery, review generation, and prospect research systems.

## What We Do

Mahesha Automation builds custom AI systems for roofing, HVAC, and electrical contractors. Our OpenClaw agent infrastructure handles repetitive back-office work so owners can focus on running their business.

## Core Systems

### 1. Lead Response Automation
Instant qualification and response to inbound leads from websites, Google LSA, and Yelp.

### 2. Missed Lead Recovery
Resurrects cold leads from your CRM with personalized outreach and follow-up sequences.

### 3. Review Generation
Automated review requests to past customers with SMS and email campaigns.

### 4. Prospect Research
Finds and qualifies new commercial prospects using public data and AI analysis.

## OpenClaw Agent Infrastructure

Our autonomous agent framework handles multi-step workflows, makes decisions, and integrates with your existing tools (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Google Calendar, etc.).

## Target Market

Local service businesses (5-50 employees) looking to automate sales and marketing operations without hiring full-time staff.

## Tech Stack

- Python
- AI/LLM orchestration
- CRM integrations
- SMS/email automation
